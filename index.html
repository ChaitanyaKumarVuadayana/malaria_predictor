About Malaria

Key facts and prevention information

What is Malaria?

Malaria is a life-threatening disease caused by parasites transmitted through the bites of infected female Anopheles mosquitoes. It is preventable and curable.

Prevention

Key prevention methods include insecticide-treated mosquito nets, indoor residual spraying, and antimalarial medications for travelers to high-risk areas.

Global Impact

In 2022, there were an estimated 247 million cases of malaria worldwide with 619,000 deaths. The WHO African Region carries a disproportionately high share of the global malaria burden.
